Frequently Asked Questions

What is Amazeriffic?

Amazeriffic is a new program designed around making your life easier. Once downloaded, you'll be able to schedule your day, prioritize your tasks, and make the most efficient use of your time. Amazeriffic is unlike any other organizer out there!

How do you use Amazeriffic?

Amazeriffic is designed to be an intuitive application. When you download the program and install it, it will prompt you to go through a tutorial explaining how to use Amazeriffic. If you have any further quetions, just click the Help button at the top of the program.

What do I do if Amazeriffic breaks?

If Amazeriffic breaks, feel free to contact our tech support. Operating around the clock, tech support would love to get a phone call or an email from you, and can help you with any issues you're experiencing. This service is free of charge, so don't hesitate to contact us.
